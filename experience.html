Work Experience

Internship |2025 - 2026| Alpine Energy

Working in the engineering design sector, the first half of my placement was with the Overhead Lines Design team (OHL) and the second with the Electrical Protection team. In the OHL Team, I generated a Bill of Materials and compliance-checking modules in Neara that automates AS/NZS Standards validation against a project and reports non-compliance in a user-friendly visualisation. My work also included designing overhead line sections, operating surveying equipment, and investigating in-house software for use to the team. I put effort into ensuring the interns from other sectors were included in learning opportunities that arose and organized a factory tour for interns and other interested colleagues.
Key learnings from my experience in the OHL team was understanding the standards used in engineering design, exposure to common tools and components used, and observing collaboration between Engineering, Field Services, and planning teams.

While working with the Electrical Protection team, I reviewed as-built substation diagrams, designed the technical layout for a weather sensor instalment and distribution transformer, and proposed Fault Passage Indicator (FPI) relay settings. Here I developed skills in interpreting single-line diagrams and increasing my awareness of safety risks associated with transformers and substation equipment.
